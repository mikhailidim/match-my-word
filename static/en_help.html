Match My Word

This tinny application is an homage to the author of Wordle. However, the purpose is precisely the opposite. This application finds words that match the pattern.
To find words start typing, and it will show combinations from the dictionary. If you have no letter for the position, use "." (period) or space. You won't see any results until you have three letters or a letter plus two placeholders.

A
- Matching word
A
- Your letter
X
- No combinations are found.

Toolbar controls

EN/RU - Switches languages to search.
 -/+  - Control the length of the search mask. 16 is the max
  ?   - Shows this message.
